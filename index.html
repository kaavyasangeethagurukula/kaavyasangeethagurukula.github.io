"ಕೆರೆಯ ನೀರನು ಕೆರೆಗೆ ಚೆಲ್ಲಿ" Music Gurukul offering free music education to ALL where you can stay and learn (Kavysangeetha) free of charge, attend classes, workshops etc
Vision is to guide,mentor and shape career for students who would want to pursue music with penance like practice and graduate out of the Gurukul as highly proficient, socially & morally responsible artists/musicians.
